Privacy Policy for Google Extension - HighLight

Introduction

The HighLight extension allows users to highlight selected text on a webpage with a variety of colors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use this Extension, how we use that information, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

The Extension itself does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. The Extension only interacts with the text you select on a webpage for the purpose of highlighting. However, it may store certain preferences locally within your browser using Chrome's local storage.

Specifically, the Extension may store:

  • Highlighting Preferences: The selected highlight color is stored temporarily during the browsing session but is not retained after the session ends.
  • Selected Text: When you select text, it is highlighted with the chosen color, but the selected text is not saved or transmitted anywhere.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: The Extension listens for keydown events (e.g., "Ctrl + H") to trigger the text highlighting function.

Use of Data

The Extension uses local resources within your browser to:

  • Highlight selected text: Using the “Ctrl + H” shortcut, the Extension highlights the selected text with a random color from the predefined list.
  • Remove existing highlights: If selected text has already been highlighted, the Extension will remove the existing highlight before applying a new one.

This data is not persisted beyond the current browser session and is not accessible after the session is closed.

Data Retention

All settings and preferences are stored temporarily within your browser. The Extension does not transmit any data to external servers or databases. Highlighting data is cleared once you close or reload the webpage.

Third-Party Services

This Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking services, or external APIs that collect or transmit your personal data.

User Control

You have full control over the settings and behavior of this Extension. You can:

  • Disable or remove highlights: By selecting highlighted text again and using the Extension, previous highlights will be removed.
  • Uninstall the Extension: Uninstalling the Extension will remove all functionality, and no further data will be stored.

Security

All data used by the Extension is stored securely within Chrome’s local storage. The Extension does not transmit data over the internet, minimizing the risk of unauthorized access. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and browser.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any changes are made, we will notify users by updating the date at the top of the policy. Continued use of the Extension after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
