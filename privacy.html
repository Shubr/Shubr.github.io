HighLight Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Our extension primarily focuses on enhancing your browsing experience by allowing you to highlight text with custom colors. We only collect the following minimal information: Checkbox state: Whether or not you have checked the random color checkbox. Selected color preference: If you manually choose a highlight color using the extension. We do not collect or process any personal data such as your name, email address, or browsing history.

How we collect info

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes: Saving Preferences: To save your color selection and checkbox state so that you can continue using the extension with your previously selected settings. This information is stored locally on your device and is never shared with external servers or third-party services.

Data Storage and Security

All data, such as your checkbox state and selected highlight color, is stored locally in your browser's storage. This means that the data remains private to your browser and device, and we do not transmit or share this information with any external servers or parties. We take appropriate security measures to safeguard your data. However, we cannot guarantee complete security, especially in cases where unauthorized access to your device occurs.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not integrate with any third-party services or track your usage through external analytics tools. It operates fully within the confines of your browser.
